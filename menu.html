The Menu

A menu made
to move you

The King Club isn't just a place to eat. It's where food and culture collide - where meals are moments and every cocktail tells a story. Our menu reflects our rhythm. Bold flavours, premium ingredients, and indulgent portions - made for sharing celebrating, and savouring. From flame-grilled classics and signature platters to handcrafted cocktails and timeless desserts, every item is curated to elevate your time with us. Come hungry. Leave changed.
